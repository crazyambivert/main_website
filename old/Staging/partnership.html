Creating Futures is in its infancy, and we need your support, involvement and contribution.
We are looking for help in the following categories:

  • IT equipment, such as desktop computers/laptops, mouse, keyboard, printer, scanner, software licenses, overhead projector, etc.
  • Stationery, such as flip charts, markers, dusters, book, white board, paper
  • Office furniture
  • Books and training materials on software development, personal development, etc.
  • Training rooms for conducting the workshops
  • Monetary assistance
  • ...And as volunteers of Creating Futures

Irrespective of whether you are an individual, an organization, or an NGO, we welcome your partnership and support.

As an IT organisation

  • Take in interns from Creating Futures as "employees of first choice"
  • Collaborate in enhancing Creating Futures' program, based on forthcoming business demands
  • Provide short-term job contracts and post-internship placement for internal job assignments
  • Extend the training material of Creating Futures
  • Assign "Real-Life" projects that interns can work on
  • Provide infrastructure support, such as office environment and IT facility during weekends

If you wish to partner with us, please write to us at info@creatingfutures.in

As a recruitment agency

  • Place the interns who have undergone the Creating Futures internship program in suitable organizations
  • Publicize Creating Futures' programs and services to corporates
  • Support interns in taking up common aptitude tests for employment
  • Partner with Creating Futures by providing market demand and support re. preparing curriculum for interns

If you wish to partner with us, please write to us at info@creatingfutures.in

As an educational institution

  • Identify candidates to be taken in as interns by Creating Futures
  • Provide classrooms/ AV rooms for conducting training

For more information, please write to us at info@creatingfutures.in

How can a corporate house utilize our services?

  • Outsource an internal project to Creating Futures
  • Order a software product from us
  • Partner with Creating Futures to identify and train freshers to be absorbed into your organization

If you wish to avail our services, please write to us at info@creatingfutures.in

As a volunteer

  • Support us with your time and skills in the following areas:
    • Software Development
    • Training (Technical and soft skills)
    • Training Content Developers
    • Designers (Usability, Collaterals, etc)
    • Accounting
    • Marketing
  • You can contribute your time for a specific project, for specific hours, or for a longer period of engagement

For more information, please write to us at volunteer@creatingfutures.in
