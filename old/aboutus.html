About Us
Creating Futures is a non-profit and non-government organization primarily focusing in the IT and ITES sector. It aims to create employment opportunities for the educated rural and urban youth from needy and economically under-privileged background by employing them and providing them with real-life experiences, as well as training them in various skills to make them highly capable corporate-ready resources. This initiative includes:

  • Technology-based project experience and training
  • Soft-skill training and development
  • End-to-End software development lifecycle program
  • Business and corporate-enabled programs

What we do
There are a large number of students graduating each year from various colleges. Close to 30% of these students are engineers. Of these 40% of them do not get employment in the year after they graduate. These high levels of unemployment lead to poverty, which in turn creates a cycle of debt. The reason: colleges being academically-oriented, do not focus on the skills required to perform efficiently in a corporate environment. It is students from rural areas and those from under-privileged economic background who are most affected due to their lack of exposure to the enhanced skills that companies look for. Some such skills required in the IT sector today include:

  • Good communication skills
  • Business awareness
  • Interpersonal skills
  • Planning and organizing
  • Analytical skills
  • Delivery excellence
  • Ability to think critically
  • Ability to work productively in a team


Our Mission, Vision, and Objective
Creating Futures is a non-profit organization in the IT space aiming to facilitate and provide employment opportunities for the needy, under-privileged or marginalized educated youth

  • Provide employment opportunities for the educated youth
  • Creating a network of resource to reduce unemployment


Goals and Objectives
Creating Futures will aim to facilitate various training programs and real-life experiences that will help them develop the required skills and provide them employment opportunities through tie-ups with corporates and recruitment agencies.

  • Increase the career options of students by providing suitable training and experience that includes a rigorous real-life work environment.
  • Help students develop technical skills and soft skills that meet industry standards.
  • Provide students with hands-on learning.
  • Provide students with the ability to make the connection between classroom instruction and real business environment by applying knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems.
  • Create an eco-system of resources with focused skill development for students
  • With more than 70% of our population living in rural areas or being a part of the urban poor population, focus will be on these groups of students.
  • Enroll graduate students with the intention of giving them the necessary training and experience, thus enabling them to be employed.
